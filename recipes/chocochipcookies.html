Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe!

Descriptions

Hello! This page is about making Chocolate Chip Cookies. Although it might contain a little more ingredients than my previous recipe in regards to the basic mash potato, it should still be just as simple! Belows the list of ingredients you might need to make these delicious cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

After you've grabbed all those ingredients, now the time to finally start making these delicious cookies!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 miniutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
